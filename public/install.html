🍎 iPhone / iPad (Safari)

2

3
Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
4

🤖 Android (Chrome)

1
Open this page in Chrome
2
Tap the ⋮ three-dot menu in the top right
3
Tap "Install App" or "Add to Home Screen"
4
Tap Install to confirm
